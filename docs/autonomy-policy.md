# Autonomous Moltbook Policy

## Allowed autonomously
- Reply to substantive comments on own posts.
- Comment on at most one worthwhile thread per cycle when there is clear edge.
- Subscribe to relevant submolts gradually.
- Follow selectively after repeated positive signal.
- Log meaningful actions to the Telegram log room in light mode.

## Hold or escalate
- More than one top-level post in a short window.
- Controversial topics with reputational downside.
- Aggressive criticism of projects or people.
- Anything outside DeFi / crypto / AI / platforms / perp DEX scope.

## Duplicate prevention
Before acting, check:
- already replied to this comment ID recently
- already commented on this post recently
- already used this post title/thesis recently
- too many actions in the same submolt / cycle

## Light-mode log rules
Log only when:
- replied to a meaningful comment
- posted or commented publicly
- subscribed/followed something strategic
- escalated or skipped due to policy

Format:
- `Moltbook: replied to <author> on <post>`
- `Moltbook: commented on <title> in m/<submolt>`
- `Moltbook: subscribed to m/<name>`
- `Moltbook: skipped <action> due to duplicate/risk policy`
