# Priority Projects

## Ondo
- Focus: tokenized real-world assets, institutional rails, onchain access to traditional yield.
- Bull case: strong distribution narrative, credible institutional bridge, benefits from demand for compliant onchain yield.
- Bear case: regulatory and wrapper dependence, less crypto-native upside than pure DeFi experiments, value may accrue unevenly across product and token.
- Good angles: product-market fit for onchain treasury exposure, distribution advantage, whether token capture matches protocol adoption.

## Pendle
- Focus: yield stripping, fixed yield / variable yield markets, rate views expressed onchain.
- Bull case: one of the cleanest DeFi expressions of yield trading; benefits when yield dispersion and sophistication increase.
- Bear case: complexity can cap retail adoption; activity may cluster around incentive windows or a few flagship assets.
- Good angles: maturity of onchain rates market, composability, whether usage is structural or campaign-driven.

## Cicada Finance
- Focus: treat as an emerging yield/platform project until more verified internal notes exist.
- Research angle: identify exact mechanism, source of yield, trust assumptions, and dependency stack before strong claims.
- Comment style: stay curious and analytical; avoid overclaiming until mechanism clarity is high.

## Aave
- Focus: money markets, collateralized lending, DeFi base-layer credit.
- Bull case: durability, network effects, strong brand, broad collateral integrations, repeated usage across cycles.
- Bear case: governance complexity, growth can feel mature relative to newer narratives, token upside may lag protocol importance.
- Good angles: Aave as infrastructure, quality of collateral, borrow demand, moat vs newer lenders.

## Maple
- Focus: onchain credit and institutional / permissioned or curated lending exposure.
- Bull case: differentiated credit access, potential upside if onchain credit quality and underwriting reputation compound.
- Bear case: credit cycles are brutal, underwriting mistakes matter more than UI, institutional narratives can vanish during stress.
- Good angles: underwriting quality, real risk pricing, default handling, whether growth reflects durable borrower demand.
