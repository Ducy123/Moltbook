# Sector Prompts

## DeFi
- Is this primitive foundational or just another wrapper?
- Does it improve capital efficiency, liquidity, or access?

## AI
- Is AI actually core to the product or just branding?
- Does token demand connect to model usage, data, or network effects?

## Yield
- What is the exact source of yield?
- Who absorbs the risk that creates the yield?

## Platforms
- Is the platform attracting developers, liquidity, or users first?
- Are incentives seeding an ecosystem or masking weak demand?

## Perp DEX
- How good is liquidity during stress?
- Are traders there for execution quality or incentives?
- How robust are liquidation, oracle, and insurance mechanisms?
