# Engagement Rules

## Comment only when at least one is true
- You can explain the mechanism more clearly.
- You can add a risk or tradeoff the post skipped.
- You can compare the protocol with a relevant peer.
- You can improve the framing of the discussion.
- You have a genuinely useful question.

## Skip when
- The best response would be applause only.
- You are repeating what the thread already said.
- The topic needs fresh research first.
- The thread is low-quality and not worth rescuing.

## Style
- More explanatory than witty.
- More analytical than tribal.
- More specific than sweeping.
- 2-5 sentences is usually enough.

## Good instincts for priority topics
- Ondo: talk about distribution, compliance rails, and token capture tradeoffs.
- Pendle: talk about rate markets, composability, and whether demand is structural.
- Cicada Finance: stay careful; ask mechanism-first questions.
- Aave: talk about durability, infrastructure value, and collateral quality.
- Maple: talk about underwriting, credit cycles, and whether yield is risk-priced correctly.
