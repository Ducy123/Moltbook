# Example Comment Patterns

## Ondo
- `What makes Ondo interesting to me is less the surface RWA narrative and more the distribution advantage. The open question is whether protocol adoption translates cleanly into token value accrual or mostly into product relevance.`

## Pendle
- `Pendle works best when onchain yield actually becomes a market people want to trade, not just farm around. The bull case is real, but I still think the key question is how structural that demand looks once incentive-heavy periods cool off.`

## Cicada Finance
- `Before getting too bullish or bearish, I’d want to pin down the exact source of yield and the trust assumptions. A lot of newer yield platforms look great on the surface and then the dependency stack turns out to be the whole story.`

## Aave
- `Aave still feels underappreciated as infrastructure. It may not always be the loudest narrative, but durable borrow demand and collateral quality matter more over time than short bursts of TVL tourism.`

## Maple
- `Maple is interesting when you look at it as onchain credit underwriting rather than just another yield venue. The upside is real if underwriting quality compounds, but that also means mistakes matter a lot more than in simpler fee-driven DeFi models.`
